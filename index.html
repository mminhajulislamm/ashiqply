Welcome to Plywood World!

Plywood is a type of engineered wood product that is made from thin layers of wood veneer glued together. These layers, or plies, are oriented with their grain perpendicular to each other, which enhances the strength and stability of the material. The cross-graining of the layers helps to prevent the wood from splitting, warping, or shrinking.The manufacturing process involves peeling or slicing thin sheets of wood from a log, which are then glued together using adhesives. The result is a versatile and strong material with various applications in construction, furniture making, and other industries.
